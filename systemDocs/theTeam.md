# The Team

We are a dedicated team of three Information Technology students specializing in Cyber Security. Our current project is the **Attendance Management System (AMS)**.

## Project Focus: Security in Software Engineering

While AMS functions foundationally as a comprehensive Software Engineering project—which we have built from the ground up—our primary objective and distinguishing focus is its **Cyber Security** aspect. 

Our goal is to ensure that while the core software engineering components are robust and fully functional, the system's architecture, data handling, and operational integrity are heavily fortified according to advanced security principles. Our documentation and discussions will predominantly highlight these security measures, though we will also detail the underlying software engineering framework.
